[ Infants | Children | Males | Females | Legend ] The RDA is a general guideline for people to know how much of each nutrients they require, depending on their sex, age and weight. These figures apply to most healthy people and do not cover special nutritional needs arising from metabolic disorders, chronic diseases, injuries, premature birth, and other medical conditions, and drug therapies.
