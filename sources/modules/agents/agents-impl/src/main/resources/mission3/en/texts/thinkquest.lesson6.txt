The FOOD Files: You and Your Food
Lesson 6
Objectives:
At the end of the one-week lesson, the students will be able to: Approximate Time of Completion: 2 Weeks Target Audience: 9th-12th Health Students

1. Share information about specific foods 2. Understand what they eat and from where it comes Materials Needed:  Computers with internet connections  Presentation software (optional)  Drawing software (optional) Activities:  In this lesson, we will be using the sections Food Facts (http://library.advanced.org/tq1997/11960/facts/index.htm) and Food Fun! (http://library.advanced.org/tq1997/11960/fun/index.htm)  Each student should get the information about five foods (some may have a little more than others)  The students must each present three of these foods to the class, explaining what they chose and why they chose what they did Assessment:  Thoroughness of presentation
