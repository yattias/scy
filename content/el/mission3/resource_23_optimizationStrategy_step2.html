Presentation of the optimization strategy: second step

Until now, Mr. Adam considered the three criteria as equally important. However, in a discussion he had with a friend, who is more knowledgeable about cars, made him realize that the ‘guarantee’ is the most important criterion and that the cost is more important than fuel consumption. Thus, while he should take into consideration all three criteria, he must give somehow more emphasis on the guarantee (10 on a scale of 10), then the cost (8 on a scale of 10) and finally the fuel consumption (5 on a scale of 10) of each car.
