The 'Healthy Pizza' mission addresses the need of healthy meals at school canteens/cafeterias around the world. Today, children in Europe and the United States consume more food products high in sugar, salt and fat content than thirty years ago. This led to the increase of many health problems including obesity, diabetes, high blood pressure, high cholesterol, heart related disease and other diet-related problems. Thus, the purpose of the Canteen Cuisine mission is to actively engage you in practices related to the canteen/cafeteria cuisine of your school. You will be involved in creating a healthy pizza for your school canteen/cafeteria while taking into consideration the nutritional value of the ingredients, the human digestive system, diet-related health issues and daily exercise.

In order to complete the mission, you will follow a stepwise approach. To move on to the next step, you must first complete the previous one. However, you will have the freedom to revisit the steps you have already completed whenever you wish.

 


Mission learning goals
In this mission you will:

  • identify health problems as a result of consuming unhealthy food products available at school canteens/cafeterias.
  • identify and describe the different nutrients, their function and their daily required quantity.
  • read and understand the label of ingredients on food items.
  • perform simple calculations and calculate the nutritional value of a certain quantity of a product.
  • reflect on the concept of the food pyramid (old and new) and the different groups of food products.
  • reflect on my own eating habits (health passport), the importance of a balanced diet and the long-term consequences when consuming unhealthy food.
  • describe the nutritional needs of a person (e.g. proteins, carbohydrates, calcium, minerals, vitamins, etc.).
  • explain the caloric needs of a person based on the basic energy requirements (BER) and the extra energy requirements (EER).
  • describe the digestive system, its functions and processes.
  • identify health issues and describe ways to combat/avoid them.
  • create a healthy pizza based on the information attained from the previous activities (optimization strategy).
