2.4: Optional: Ingredients Table
In the occasion of available time and appropriate equipment (mobile devices/mobile phones), students can collect the nutritional value information of the pizza ingredients and complete the table below on their own. Groups of students can visit local supermarket/grocery stores with mobile storing devices or carry out a web-search in order to collect the information. In case mobile devices are not available, they can take paper notes. Since there are so many ingredients on the table it will be very time consuming to find the information for all them. There are two alternatives to this problem. Groups of students could look up the info of different groups of ingredients (e.g. 5-10 ingredients per group) or the teacher can provide a semi-complete table and have students complete the missing information. The table will provide students with an authentic, hands-on activity and can be used in Activity 7 during the optimization strategy.
