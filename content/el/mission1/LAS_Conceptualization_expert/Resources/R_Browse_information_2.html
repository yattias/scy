Critically examine and compare resources

Having found a number of resources with the appropriate information, you need to collect and organize your findings in an efficient way. It might help you to follow these guidelines:

Read

First, you have to read the resources you have found carefully. Of course you may skip sections that have no relation with any of your research questions.

Summarize

Then you have to summarize the information that you need. Instead of writing a summary in regular text-format, you can also try to make a list of keywords or ‘key-sentences’. Do NOT copy-paste entire paragraphs from existing texts.

Organise data

Sometimes it is wise to organise particular information in tables (e.g. lists of appliances and their attributes, etc.).
