Reflection assignment

The goal of this assignment is to take a serious look at the concepts and the skills you will have to deal with during this mission. Score this Rubric for your situation right now and you will score it one more time at the end of the mission, so that you will have an idea of your own development. Be honest. This task does not affect the grade(s) you will get for this mission in any way, as long as you take it seriously.

The assignment:

  1. In the left column you will find a number of skills and concepts.
    Read the descriptions in the three columns on the right (LEVEL 1, LEVEL 2 and LEVEL 3).
  2. Indicate which description is most valid for your situation right now, by marking a place on the scale with a cross. E.g. a score of 30% would mean that you are somewhere between level 1 and 2. And a score of 85% would mean that the description under level 3 is quite accurate for you.
