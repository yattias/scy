How do you present your findings in your design group?

You have to present the main findings of your expert group to your design group. The concept-map you have (or you are going to) produced will be an important instrument to tell your classmates about everything that you have learned.

But, to present your conclusions, you should argue for by giving some information about the experiments and reasoning that you have done. So, following the same steps you have done yourself, prepare a presentation (poster, slides...) about your research questions and hypotheses, describe the (virtual or real) experiments you have conducted, and summarize the main results and conclusions. Then, return to your design group and present your conclusions.

The presentation to your design group should take no longer than 10 minutes. Be ready to answer additional questions when necessary.
