Write a report for the mayor

You have learnt a lot of things during this mission. You have become an expert yourself, you have exchanged your expert knowledge with the other members of your design group, and together you have designed a CO2-friendly house. Now your task is to write an individual report to present BioEnergy’s whole study to the mayor of your city. The report has to be both informative and easy to read, so that the mayor (and potential inhabitants) of your city will be able to understand it.

Your report will consist of (at least) the following components:

Introduction

Why reduce global CO2-levels?

Explain the potential dangers of rising CO2-levels. You may use your concept-map to support your text.

Houses and CO2-emissions

Explain the main factors that contribute to the carbon footprint of a house. These are the issues that the builders and inhabitants of a house will have to deal with. Make sure to incorporate information from all three expert domains. Again, you may include (expert) concept-maps, information from existing resources (re-written in your own words) and other documents in your report.

BioEnergy's proposal for a CO2-friendly house

Present your final design. Also describe the reasoning behind  the choices that were made by your design group.

List of resources

Appendix

As an appendix, you will add a concise report on three experiments that you, or another member of your design group, has performed. For each experiment, describe the research question, hypothesis, experimental procedure, results and conclusions. You can also add other findings or documents you have produced during the mission.

You can also make some comments about the CO2-friendly house(s) produced by other design groups.
