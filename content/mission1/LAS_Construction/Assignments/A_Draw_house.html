Draw your own CO2-friendly house

After each member of your group has become an expert in a particular field of knowledge, it is time to actually start drawing your own house. Halfway through the mission you will make an inventory of expert solutions, and make some first choices for your own design. Maybe you have already done that.

Please remember, the reduction of CO2-emissions is the central issue. At the same time, your house has to provide room and a reasonable level of comfort for a family of four members (two parents and two children).

Use the technical drawing tool to show what your house looks like. The tool can be used to make 3D images, side views and top views, as well as floor plans of the house interior.
