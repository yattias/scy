24: Criteria table (Pizza optimization first step)

In order to apply the optimization strategy in the context of a healthy pizza, you should follow a process similar to the one followed by Mr. Adam when purchasing a car. Before you begin, however, first you need to recall all the ingredients you selected during the first and second pizza simulation (activities 1 and 21) and create a table listing the amount of nutrients (fats, carbohydrates, protein, etc.) and energy (in kcal) of each of the pizza ingredient you chose.

Now you will have to score each ingredient you selected up to this point so that you exalt all the criteria (fats, carbohydrates, proteins and costs) on a common scale. After you have finished, exchange the table with one of your group members for peer-assessment.

What to do next: After you complete this activity, please proceed with the "Criteria Weight Table" activity.
