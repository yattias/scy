7.2: Optimization strategy
In the next activity you will study an example of the optimization strategy, which will help you choose the healthiest pizza ingredients. The optimization strategy is a method used to make a decision when more than one option is available. The strategy calls for a two-step process. During the first step, the parameters of each option are identified and scored on a common scale, based on their characteristics. During the second step, all parameters are weighted based on their importance and multiplied with their scale score. The total score for each option is obtained by summing up all parameter scores of each option. The best option has the highest score.
