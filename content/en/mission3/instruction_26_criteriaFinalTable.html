26: Compare scores (final table)

Now that you have defined your criteria, and assigned weights to each of them, you can calculate the final scores of the pizza ingredients you selected up to this point. In order to do so, follow the instructions bellow.

In order to get the final scores, you need to drag and drop both tables you just created into the Criteria Final Table.

  1. First, drag and drop the Criteria Table. Simply click and hold on the ELO icon, drag it over the table and drop it. All the data should now appear in the Criteria Final Table.
  2. Then, drag and drop the Criteria Weight Table in the Criteria Final Table in the same manner.
  3. Now you should be able to see both datasets and the final scores for each of your ingredients in the Criteria Final Table.

What are the most healthy alternatives? In the next activity, you are going to create your final, optimized pizza based on the scores you just calculated.

What to do next: After you complete this activity, please return to the mission map and proceed with the "My Optimized Healthy Pizza" and the "My Optimized Healthy Pizza" activity.
