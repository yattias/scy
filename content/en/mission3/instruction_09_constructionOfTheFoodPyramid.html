09: Food pyramid simulation

The simulation below provides the structure of the (new) food pyramid and several food products. Your job is to place each food product in the appropriate category. In case you misplace a product, the simulator will let you know. However, it will not tell you which product was misplaced; you must find it on your own. Good luck!
