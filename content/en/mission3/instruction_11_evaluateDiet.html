11: Evaluate your diet

Evaluate your eating and drinking habits in terms of the recommendations of the food pyramid. Write a short text (50 words maximum) in which you address the questions below. This text will be part of your Health Passport.

  • Does your diet include food products from all categories of the food pyramid?
  • Are the amounts of food (from each category) in line with the recommendations of the food pyramid?
  • How could you improve your diet to become more healthy?

What to do next: After you complete this activity, please return to the mission map and proceed with the "Energy Fact Sheet" and the "Energy Fact Sheet" activity.
