Part 1: Introduction to a healthy pizza

Activities:
1.1: Create a pizza.
1.2: Video on unhealthy food products at school.
1.3: The benefits of eating a healthy pizza.
1.4: Food and exercise diary.
1.4.1: Your personal food and exercise diary (optional).
1.4.2: Create a food and exercise diary.
________________________________________________

Description of Activity 1: The purpose of activity 1 is to make you aware of unhealthy dietary products available at your school canteens/cafeterias, focusing particularly on pizza. In addition, you will create your own/imaginary food and exercise diary in order to investigate the average food consumption of a human being.

General learning goals
In this activity you will:

  • collaborate with peers (share tasks/information, collaborate on experiments, make decision together, etc.).
  • present in the presence of a large group (partners and/or classmates).
  • write individual reports and assignments.

Domain-specific learning goals
In this activity you will:

  • learn about health problems as a result of consuming unhealthy food products available at your school canteens/cafeterias.
  • learn about the benefits of consuming a healthier pizza.
  • learn how to create a food and exercise diary.
