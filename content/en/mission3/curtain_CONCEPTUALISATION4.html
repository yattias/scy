The digestive system: During the previous activities you have learned about nutrients and their function in the human body. As you remember, the overall goal of this mission is to create a healthy pizza. But if you are eating your pizza, how do the nutrients enter your bloodstream? In this part of the mission you will learn about the different organs of the digestive system and how they work.

Learning goals
In this part of the mission you will:

  • learn about the basic anatomy of the digestive system.
  • learn about the function of each organ of the digestive system, especially in terms of (a) the secretion of digestive juices and (b) the absorption of nutrients (including water).
