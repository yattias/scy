1.4.1: Your personal food and exercise diary (optional)
You probably think you have a reasonable idea of what you eat and drink on an average day. But do you know how many grams of food you actually consume? Or how many litres of fluids you drink? Finally, how much do you exercise? Now is the time to find out. For the duration of one day, you are going to keep track of everything you eat and drink. You are going to put this information in a food and exercise diary (see the example below).

  1. Note down everything you eat or drink on one day (from midnight to midnight).
  2. Not only write down the name of a particular food or drink product, but also note the quantity you have consumed (in grams or litres).
  3. If you are not sure about the weight of a particular food item, you can either check the package or use a (kitchen) scale or measuring cup to measure it. Sometimes you can find the information you need on the internet. Once you start doing this, you will develop a certain feeling about how much things weigh (e.g. an average sized glass is about 200 mL).

Your food contains energy, which is used by your body for movement, maintaining a constant body temperature and various other purposes. You will learn more about the energy your body spends in activity 4. To make your diary complete, you will also note down everything you do during a day.

  1. Note down everything you do in one day (from midnight to midnight).
  2. Not only write down what you are doing, but also how long you have been doing it (in minutes).
  3. Even when you are not really exercising, but just attending class or sleeping (or both at the same time), your body consumes energy. So, you also have to note these kinds of activities.
  4. If you are performing the same activity twice during one hour (e.g. walking to the video store, and 20 minutes later walking back to your house) you can of course add these two values up.

Check the example of a food and exercise diary to see how you write down the information. Please note that the information you put in your diary is private. You do not have to share it with others.
