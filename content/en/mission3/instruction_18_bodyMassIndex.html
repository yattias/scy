18: Body mass index

1. Use this formula to calculate your BMI.

 

If you do not know how to use this formula, follow these steps:

  • Take your height in meters.
  • Multiply this value by itself.
  • Divide your weight (in kg) by the outcome of the previous step.
  • The outcome is your BMI.

2. Enter your BMI in the health passport.
