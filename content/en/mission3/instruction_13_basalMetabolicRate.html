13: Basal metabolic rate (BMR)

1. To calculate the BMR you need your age, weight and lenght. First, write these numbers down in your Health Passport.

2. Use the following formula to calculate your BMR:

BMR = (10.0 x weight) + (6.25 x length) – (5.0 x age) + 5        (if you are a boy)

BMR = (10.0 x weight) + (6.25 x length) – (5.0 x age) – 161    (if you are a girl)

[where weight is measured in kilograms (kg), lenght in centimetres (cm) and age in years (y)]

3. Your BMR will be used for your Health Passport.
