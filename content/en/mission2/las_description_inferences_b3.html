In order to test whether your previously stated hypotheses are correct, you will run experiments. This is where you can complete the following assignments:

1. Data collection. Open your ‘Hypotheses’ ELO and collect evidence related to your hypotheses, using a prey-predator model.
2. Data analysis. The modelling tool allows you to produce data tables and graphs that can be analysed in order to make inferences.
3. Generating inferences. Open the ‘Inference’ ELO and make your inferences.
 
After generating your inferences, open the mission map and move next to the Problem solution space.

Learning goals

After successfully completing these assignments, you will be able to:

• plan experiments for testing your hypotheses;
• model biological processes;
• analyse, visualise and interpret data gathered from your experiments;
• relate your research findings to your hypotheses and generate inferences.
