Do the experiment. Open your ‘Hypotheses’ ELO and collect evidence about your hypotheses using this model about influence of nutrients on biomass.

  1. First, look at the model.
  2. Next, try to draw graphs that relate to your hypotheses. To do this, click on the tab "Graph", select the appropriate variables and click "run simulation".
  3. Finally, produce a data table by using your experimental procedure to select variables and run the simulation.

Note that you should manipulate only the independent variables which you have already identified in the description of your experimental procedure.
