Experimental procedure

1. Take 20 grams of sand, 20 grams of fragments of limestone and 20 grams of fragments of granite and rinse them in tap water to remove dust and dirt.
2. Rinse the samples with distilled water.
3. Take 12 beakers (each 100 ml).
4. Weigh out the samples into the beakers: 5 g of sand into beakers 1-4 (total 20 g), 5 g of limestone into beakers 5-8 (total 20 g), and 5 g of granite into beakers 9-12 (total 20 g).
5. Add 50 ml distilled water to each of the beakers (total 600 ml).
6. Stir the mixtures for about 1 minute.
7. Leave the samples on a table for 5 minutes before measuring the pH.
8. Measure the pH of one sample of sand mixture, one sample of limestone mixture, and one sample of granite mixture. Use the Vernier pH sensor. Write your data into the table in the SCY-Lab Data processing tool. You may write the results on a piece of paper first, if necessary.
9. Add 1 ml of sulphuric acid solution A (0.0001 M) to three beakers – one containing sand mixture (beaker 1), one containing limestone mixture (beaker 5), and one containing granite mixture (beaker 9).
10. Add 10 ml of sulphuric acid solution A (0.0001 M) to the next three beakers – one containing sand mixture (beaker 2), one containing limestone mixture (beaker 6), and one containing granite mixture (beaker 10).
11. Add 1 ml of sulphuric acid solution B (0.01 M) to the next three beakers – one containing sand mixture (beaker 3), one containing limestone mixture (beaker 7), and one containing granite mixture (beaker 11).
12. Add 10 ml of sulphuric acid solution B (0.01 M) to the final three beakers – one containing sand mixture (beaker 4), one containing limestone mixture (beaker 8), and one containing granite mixture (beaker 12).
13. Measure the pH of the water in all beakers with the Vernier pH sensor. Write your data into the table in the SCY-Lab Data processing tool. You may write the results on a piece of paper first, if necessary.

Important notes:

• Please test your Vernier pH sensor with standardized buffer solution before starting to measure.
• Please be patient, sometimes it takes a few minutes for the pH reading to stabilize.
• Please stir the Vernier pH sensor gently in distilled water and dry it with a paper towel before taking each measurement.
