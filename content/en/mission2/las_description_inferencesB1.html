In order to test if your previously stated hypotheses are correct, you have to run some experiments. Here you can compose the following tasks:

  1. Experimental procedure. First you have to plan your experiment. For that, open the “Experimental procedure” ELO and follow all the instructions related to the ELO.
  2. Data collection. After your experimental procedure is finished, you can start performing your experiments. For that, open the ELO “SCYDynamics model” and follow the guidelines about conducting experiments.
  3. Data analysis. Open Data processing tool and analyze the data collected from your experiments.
  4. Formulating inferences. Open the “Inference” ELO and make your inferences.

After formulating your inferences, open the mission map and move next to the “Solution” space.
