The video report illustrates your inquiry work. You need to make a video while working through one of the mission's topics. This video should be edited and uploaded to YouTube. You can import the link to the video in SCY-Lab.

More information on the video report can be found in the drawers.

 

Learning goals

After successfully completing the assignment, you are able to:

• represent your inquiry work in visual form;
• make a video report;
• edit and upload videos.
