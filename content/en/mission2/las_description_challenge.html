Learning goals

After successful completion of this mission, you will be able to act as a researcher and:

• identify problems from narratives and videos based on everyday situations;
• search and critically analyse background information;
• formulate hypotheses that relate dependent and independent variables;
• plan experiments for testing your hypotheses;
• carry out real or virtual experiments;
• analyse, visualise and interpret data gathered from your experiments;
• relate your research findings to the hypotheses and initial problem;
• explain the relations among different parameters of an aquatic ecosystem;
• use the mission-specific concepts in biology, physics and chemistry correctly;
• effectively organise your work;
• plan, monitor and properly evaluate your learning process.
