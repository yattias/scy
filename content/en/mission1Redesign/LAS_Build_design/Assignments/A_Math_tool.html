With this tool you are going to work with geometrical calculations relevant for house design. Working with this tool you will get two main tasks:

1. Explain how the surface area to volume ratio changes with the size and shape of a specific 3 dimensional object:

  • Choose the 3D tab.
  • Calculate the empty parameters of one big and one small sphere. Add your results to the computation sheet by pressing 'Add'.
  • Calculate the empty parameters of one big and one small rectangular prism. Add your results to the computation sheet by pressing 'Add'.
  • Sort the computation sheet ascending according to sa/v - ratio
  • Reflect on the following: Which object has the least ratio and which has the highest? What does this mean when designing a house in terms of heat loss?


2. Design the ground floor of your house:
  • Choose the 2D tab
  • Create a ground floor with a surface area 120 square meters by using at least one triangle, one rectangle and one circle.
  • Use the calculator to calculate the surface area for each shape and the summarize them in the computation sheet on the right.
  • Use the drawing of the ground floor to construct a drawing of your house in Google Sketchup. You can export your ground floor by clicking the house image in the top left corner.
