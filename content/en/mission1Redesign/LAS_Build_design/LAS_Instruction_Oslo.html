Design your own CO2-friendly house

After each member of your group has become an expert in a particular field of knowledge, It is now time to actually start designing your own CO-2-friendly house. Each member of your design group has become an expert in a particular field of knowledge. You have shared these differents kinds of expertise and made decisions on the basis of this shared knowledge for the design of your CO-2 friendly house. So nearly all preparations have been done, and the 'real' design work can begin!

In this activity you will first do some geometrical tasks. What you are going to do is to work on surface areas and volumes for different 3 dimensional geometrical shapes. Then we want you to create the ground floor of your house using different 2 dimensional geometrical shapes. When the latter task is done you can export an image of this ground floor to Google Sketchup where you finalize a 3 dimensional drawing of your house. Make sure that your design is consistent with the choices you have made before!
