Run experiments

You may have prepared (and realized) some real experiments but since you belong to the Energy expert group, you may also carry out some virtual experiments using the Energy to CO2 emission converter (also called Converter).

You can read the manual to understand what you can do with this simulation. At the end of your experiment, save your data set and it will appear on your SCY-Lab screen. Then, it will be the time to process your data.
