Here are some guidelines for preparing your presentation:

  • The presentation itself should take no longer than 10 minutes.
  • The presentation may be done by your whole design group, or by one or two representatives (consult your teacher).
  • If you choose to hold the presentation with two or more people, make sure that each speaker has a specific part of the presentation for which he or she is responsible.
  • When you are finished with the preparatory work, ask your teacher for some final feedback.
  • Practice your presentation a few times in your design group.
