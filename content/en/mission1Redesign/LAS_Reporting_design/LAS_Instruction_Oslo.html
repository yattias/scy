Give a presentation of your CO-2 friendly house

At the end of the mission, you have to present the final design of your CO2-friendly house to the other design groups.

You should include information about the following themes:

  • a presentation of the house
  • relevant knowledge from the four expert groups
  • choice of energy input
  • an argumentation for the shape of the house
  • energy loss
  • choice of building material and insulation
  • cost and realism of your CO-2 friendly house
  • Remember to bring in illustration of important points
