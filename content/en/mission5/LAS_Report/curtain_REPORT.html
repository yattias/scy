You have studied the effects of exercise and energy drinks on blood glucose concentration. In this final part of the mission you will present your work in a report. It is very important that your report is complete and understandable. If it is not, other people (like the teacher or your classmates) may not be convinced by your results.

 

Learning goals

In this part of the mission you will learn:

  • how to describe your simulations
  • how to outline the main findings of your research
  • how to present your work
