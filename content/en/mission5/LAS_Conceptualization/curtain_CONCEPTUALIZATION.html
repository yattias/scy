Now that you have learned about the role of glucose in your body, let's return to the question of the effect of energy drinks. What do you think will happen in your body after drinking a can of energy drink? In this part of the mission you will predict the effect of glucose intake on the concentration of glucose in your blood in different circumstances. This prediction is your 'hypothesis'.
