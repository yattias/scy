Annotated data

To keep a trace of your results, take a picture of your gel and upload it in your computer. Then, use the uploader button of the drawing tool and click anywhere in the window tool. Annotate the picture with arrows and/or small textboxes to indicate:
- which inks have been deposed on the chromatography plate,
- what are the conditions of the experiment

If you don't have a camera, use the tool to draw your gel before making your annotation.

If you want to ensure the reliability, it is better to perform several times your experiment.
