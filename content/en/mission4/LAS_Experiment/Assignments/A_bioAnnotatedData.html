Annotation on my results

Upload the picture(s) into this drawing tool by dragging your picture in the drawing tool icon. Annotate the picture with arrows and/or small textboxes to indicate:

  • which DNA-profile belongs to which donor (the sample found at the crime scene, the victim, suspect A, etc.)
  • which side of the gel contains the smaller DNA fragments, and which side contains the larger fragments,
  • which side of the gel was positively charged, and which side was negatively charged.

    • If you don't have a camera, use the tool to draw your gel before making your annotation.
    When you will have finish, ask your colleagues for feedback. To do that, open the "F" drawer on the left side of this window.
