You are not the only biologist who is solving this problem. There is a small group of biologists, who also created their biology-specific decisions. In this space, you have to give feedback on their ELO-s. Others will give feedback on your ELO also.

Your tasks are:

  1. Ask your colleagues feedback on your ELO (Field-specific decision).
  2. Give feedback on your colleagues’ ELO-s.
  3. Compose Revised field-specific decision on the basis of your last ELO and feedback.

After you have formulated the ELO Revised field-specific decision, open the mission map and move next to the Expert council’s decision space.
