In the GMO module you will get a chance to solve a dilemma situation involving genetically modified organisms (GMOs). First, read the drama. According to that story, you have to compose an ELO: Initial decision.

Guidelines for completing the ELO are given in the drawers.

When you have formulated the ELO Initial decision, open the mission map and move next to the Initial field-specific decision space.
