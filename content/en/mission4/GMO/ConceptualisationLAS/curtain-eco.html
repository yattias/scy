Now you have read the story about William and Kate and formulated an initial decision about the reasonability of growing genetically modified salmon in EU countries. Although, this problem seemed simple, there are actually several aspects, which need to be considered.

All students working with GMO module have been assigned to an expert role. There are four different roles: biologist, animal protetctor, economist and lawyer. You are a economist.

Your task is to think as a economist and formulate the ELO Initial field-specific decision based on economy aspects only. Please do not consider the aspects of other fields. Go on and explore the contents of the ELO drawers.

After you have formulated the ELO Initial field-specific decision, open the mission map and move next to the Revised field-specific space.

Learning goals:

After completion of this part of the mission:

• you can collect, organize and evaluate data on a definite subject;
• you can evaluate information about economic problems related to the application of gene technology;
• you take into account economic aspects in decision making;
• you make competent decisions on personal and societal issues;
• you can describe economical issues related to genetically modified food and feed;
