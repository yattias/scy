Now that you have designed and carried out your experiments, and have made an analysis of the results, it is time to report your findings to the attorney. Thus, you are going to write an official report with reliable and objective conclusions.

Learning goals
After completion of this part of the mission…
  • you can extract, organize and evaluate data on a definite subject.
  • you can asses information about scientific, legal, economic and ethical problems related to the application of gene technology.
  • you have acquired an overview of science disciplines and professions related to genetics and gene technology.
