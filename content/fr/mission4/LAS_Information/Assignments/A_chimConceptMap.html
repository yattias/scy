Concept Map for chemical analysis

Make a concept map related to the technique of chemical analysis that will be used in this part of the mission: the thin layer chromatography (TLC).
There is a double goal in this activity:

  • Identify and organize the concepts related to the TLC
  • Connect the TLC to the other techniques that a chemical analyst might use.

    • The concepts are already given on the side of the Cmap. You have to organize them and give a linking phrase between the concepts.
