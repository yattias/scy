The FOOD Files: You and Your Food
Evaluation Criteria for Assessment
Since this lesson can be used for any level Health-related class, the a rubric is not included and assessment is subjective based upon your population. This instructional unit could be offered to the students for a quarterly marking period. The unit in its entirety can be graded or it can be broken down into its different lesson plans. The integration of technology into a subject such as Diet, one that is essential to each and every one of us, makes the lessons flow much more smoothly. The projects to be evaluated within this unit are both academic and creatively based are listed as follows:  Compare & Contrast Essays  Discover and Document Rationale  Capturing Relevant Information  Presentating Information to a Group  Graphing and Charting skills Any teachers should be able to utilize this information within the context of Health and Diet. Teachers can also coordinate activities through the Discussion section; interschool projects are extremely viable through the use of the Internet. Any suggestions are surely welcome, as are questions. We'd all appreciate it if you share your experiences with The FOOD Files: You and Your Food
