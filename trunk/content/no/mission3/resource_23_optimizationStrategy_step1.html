As you can see in the table above, it is very difficult for Mr. Adam to choose among the three cars which one is better. Car B is the cheapest vehicle and has the lowest fuel consumption, however, it has the shortest guarantee time. Car A on the other hand, has a longer guaranty but consumes a bit more fuel and it cost more money than car B. Finally, car C has the longest guarantee but it’s much more expensive and it consumes a lot more fuel. Since, Mr. Adam wants to take into account these three criteria, you should find out which one of the three cars offers the best combination of advantages and disadvantages. Discuss with your teammates how Mr. Adam could overcome this problem.
