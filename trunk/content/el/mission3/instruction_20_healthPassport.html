20: Health passport: conclusions and recommendations

You are now going to finish your health passport. Complete the health passport with a short reflection (150 words maximum). Evaluate the balance between daily calorie intake and estimated energy requirements. Also note if there are any signs of an unhealthy lifestyle (e.g. a BMI that is too high). Formulate a few recommendations for yourself on how to improve your dietary and exercising habits (when necessary).
