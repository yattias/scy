5.1 Introduction

During the previous activities you have learned about nutrients and their function in the human body. As you remember, the overall goal of this mission is to create a healthy pizza. But if you are eating your pizza, how do the nutrients enter your bloodstream? In this activity you will learn about the different organs of the digestive system and how they work.

General learning goals
Students will be:

  • able to explain simple information to the rest of the class.

Specific learning goals
Students will be:

  • able to reproduce the basic anatomy of the digestive system.
  • able to explain the function of each organ of the digestive system, especially in terms of (a) the secretion of digestive juices and (b) the absorption of nutrients (including water).
  • able to explain that digestive juices contain certain substances (enzymes) that break down nutrients into substances that are small enough to be absorbed by the digestive system.
