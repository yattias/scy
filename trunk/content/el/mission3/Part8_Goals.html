Part 8: Create and Compare Pizzas

Activities
8.1: Creation of a healthy pizza.
8.2: Compare pizzas.
8.2.1: Compare pizzas among groups.
8.2.2: Optional: pizza taste competition.
8.3: Reflection.
________________________________________________

Description of Activity 8: Based on the optimization strategy, you will create a new healthy pizza and calculate its nutritional value. You will then compare all three pizzas you created throughout the mission. Finally, you will have the opportunity to compare and compete for the healthiest pizza among your peers.

General learning goals
In this activity you will:

  • identify a problem and formulate hypotheses.
  • organize, analyze and interpret data.
  • collaborate with peers (share tasks/information, collaborate on experiments, make decision together, etc.).
  • reflect on your own knowledge and learning goals.
  • present in the presence of a large group (partners and/or classmates).
  • write individual reports and assignments.

Domain-specific learning goals
In this activity you will:

  • create your final healthy pizza using the optimization strategy.
  • compare the pizzas you created throughout the mission and decide which is the healthiest.
  • compare your pizzas with those of other groups and compete for the healthiest and tastiest one.
  • respond to questions concerning the epistemological issues of the optimization strategy.
