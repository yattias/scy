7.2.3: Implementation of the optimization strategy in the context of a healthy pizza.
In order to apply the optimization strategy in the context of a healthy pizza, you should follow a process similar to the one followed by Mr. Adam when purchasing a car. Before you begin, however, first you need to recall all the ingredients you selected during the first and second pizza simulation (part 1.1 and 7.1.1) and create a table listing the nutrients (carbohydrates, fat, protein, etc.), energy and cost of each of the pizza ingredient you chose.
