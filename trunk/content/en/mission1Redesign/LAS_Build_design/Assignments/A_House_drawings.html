Design a CO-2 friendly house for a family of four (two parents and two children). The house should be as CO-2 friendly as possible, but at the same time provide enough room and a reasonable level of comfort for its users. Your challenge is to balance both requirements in your design.

Your design group has to produce a collection of drawings that include:

  • drawings of the exterior of the house (front view, side view and top view),
  • floor plans of the interior of the house,
  • a 3D artist’s impression.
All drawings, except for the artist’s impression, must specify dimensions (e.g. size of walls, windows, etc.).

Your house drawings can be produced in different ways:

  • using Google SketchUp (see the 'Resources' drawer),
  • using other (technical) drawing software,
  • using pencil and paper (and a ruler, to make sure that dimensions are accurate).
