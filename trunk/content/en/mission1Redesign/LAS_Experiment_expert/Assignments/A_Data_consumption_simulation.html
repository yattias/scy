Run virtual experiments

You may have prepared (and realized) some real experiments but since you belong to the Domestic use expert group, you may also carry out some virtual experiments using the Energy consumption simulation (http://bio.edu.ee/flash/electricity/)

You can read the manual to understand what you can do with this simulation (manual incorporated in the tool). At the end of your experiment, save your data set and it will appear on your SCY-Lab screen. Then, it will be the time to process your data.
