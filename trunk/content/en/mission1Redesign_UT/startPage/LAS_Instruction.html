What is the challenge?

Your task in this mission is to design a CO-2 friendly house. As a first step, you have to fully understand what this task is about and how you should approach it. This is the main goal of this activity.

You perform this activity by watching a short video that explains the concept of CO-2 emission and its consequences for life on earth. The video also indiates the importance of designing houses with as little CO-2 emission as possible, and illustrates the challenges you will be facing when designing your own CO-2 friendly house.

Use this information to write down the task in your own words.
