In order to test whether your previously stated hypotheses are correct, you will run experiments. This is where you can complete the following assignments:

1. Experimental procedure. First, you need to plan your experiment. To do that, open the ’Experimental procedure’ ELO and follow all of the instructions given in the drawers.
2. Data collection. After you plan your experimental procedure, you will conduct the experiment outside SCYLab and collect the data.
3. Data analysis. Open the Data processing tool, import and analyse the data collected from your experiments.
4. Generating inferences. Open the ‘Inferences’ ELO and make your inferences.
After generating your inferences, open the mission map and move to the Problem solution space.

 

Learning goals

After successfully completing these assignments, you will be able to:

• plan experiments for testing your hypotheses;
• carry out real experiments by using mobile measurement devices;
• analyse, visualise and interpret data gathered from your experiments;
• relate your research findings to your hypotheses and generate inferences;
• collaborate effectively with peers.
