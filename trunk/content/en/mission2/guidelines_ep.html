Guidelines for designing an experimental procedure

Here are some guidelines that might help you to design your experimental procedure - check whether the following criteria are met:

1. The list of tasks is complete. All parameters and materials are specified.
2. The list of tasks is structured so that it is easy to understand. Tasks can be organized into steps and sub-steps.
3. The variables described in the research question(s) correspond with the parameters that will be changed during the experiment.
4. Only one variable will be changed at a time.
