This is where you can create your concept map about freshwater ecosystems. Please follow all of the instructions given in the drawers. Complete your initial concept map first, and then begin with your first inquiry cycle.

Every time you finish an inquiry cycle, return here to update your concept map with the new knowledge you have gained.

 

Learning goals

After successfully completing the assignment, you will be able to:

• outline the main biotic and abiotic components in a freshwater ecosystem;
• describe relationships among the main components of a freshwater ecosystem;
• identify and describe the important factors that influence a freshwater ecosystem.
