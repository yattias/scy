Designing an experimental procedure

Plan and perform an experiment where you study the effect of the light intensity on the oxygen concentration produced by plants in water. You need to use a light sensor and an oxygen probe to measure the light intensity and the concentration of oxygen in the water.

Some tips!

  • Don’t use common energy saver bulbs! The light spectrum of energy savers is not appropriate for photosynthesis.
  • Shut the curtains and turn off the lights in your room during the experiment.
  • Collect the water from the tap very slowly in a beaker. There should be no air bubbles.
  • The water should be at room temperature (18-23 °C).
