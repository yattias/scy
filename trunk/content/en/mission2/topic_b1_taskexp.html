Experimental procedure

Two experiments that can be conducted are the following:

• measure biomass growth (during an interval of 10 time steps) with different concentrations of nitrate, and a fixed phosphate concentration; e.g. vary nitrate concentrations between 1 and 5000 μmol/l and a phosphate concentration of 3 μmol/l
• measure biomass growth (during an interval of 10 time steps) with different concentrations of phosphate, and a fixed nitrate concentration; e.g. vary phosphate concentrations between 0.1 and 20 μmol/l and a nitrate concentration of 15 μmol/l
