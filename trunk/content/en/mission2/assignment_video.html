In this mission you are going to perform up to four experiments to learn more about aquatic ecosystems. For each experiment you will record a 5 minute long video report (in a group with other students) that explains to other students the experiment you have done. Every video should contain the following chapters:

1. Explain the problem addressed by your research. Provide some general background information.

2. State clearly your research questions and hypotheses.

3. Film and describe the actual experiment and its set-up, or show the computer model you have been working on.

4. Discuss the results of your experiments and incorporate video stills of the graphs and figures you have created.

5. Discuss the main conclusions of your investigations; make some references to the general problem you were trying to solve.

Before recording your video, create an initial story board to guide you.
