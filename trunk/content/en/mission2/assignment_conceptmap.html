An aquatic ecosystem is an ecosystem located in a body of water. There live communities of organisms that are dependent on each other and on their environment. The two main types of aquatic ecosystems are marine ecosystems and freshwater ecosystems.

Make your own freshwater ecosystem as a concept map by adding the factors that control the dynamics of the system (influencing factors, dependent factors, determining factors and outline environmental functions).
