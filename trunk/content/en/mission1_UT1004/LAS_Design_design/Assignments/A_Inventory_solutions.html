What will make your house CO2-friendly?

Your goal is to design a CO2-friendly house. The past few lessons you have been working to gain a lot of knowledge about all kinds of measurements that might influence the CO2-friendliness of a house. Each of you has been working in a particular field of knowledge: thermal insulation, energy production, or domestic issues. Now the time has come to start designing (i.e., drawing, describing, simulating) YOUR OWN HOUSE.

To help you get started, your group will make an inventory of all the possible CO2-friendly solutions and adaptations that each group member knows of. Like in the brainstorm session at the beginning of the mission, you will use the schematic drawing of a house to make this inventory. Arrange all ideas around (and in) the drawing.
