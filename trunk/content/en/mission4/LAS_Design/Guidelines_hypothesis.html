What is a ‘good’ hypothesis? Here are some guidelines:

  • A hypothesis is a clear and concise statement which expresses the expected answer to a question.
  • A hypothesis has to be testable.
  • A hypothesis does not have to be correct;
    if your hypothesis turns out to be false, that doesn’t mean your research was not successful.
  • Many hypotheses are phrased as ‘if … , then …’ statements.
