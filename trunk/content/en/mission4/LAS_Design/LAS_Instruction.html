In this part of the mission you are going to justify the experimental procedure according to the techniques you have selected. You are going to discover each step of the experimental process, e.g. what are the techniques that you are going to employ, what materials do you need, and in what amounts, etcetera. In the next part of the mission you will actually perform this experiment.

Learning goals
After completion of this part of the mission,
  • you can follow an experimental process to answer a research question:
    - by formulating correct hypotheses,
    - by justifying some parts of the procedure that will be used.
