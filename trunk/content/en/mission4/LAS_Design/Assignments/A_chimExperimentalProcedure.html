Experimental procedure

You have several inks that need to be tested: one corresponds to the anonymous letter; the other ones belong to the collected pens.

To design your experimental procedure, you have to take the following steps:

1. Drag-and-drop the icon of the "Goal of the analysis" you have written before to the "Research question" field.
2. Write your anticipated results.
3. Describe the principle of the manipulation.
4. Complete the material list with the materials you are going to use during the experiment. Don't forget to specify the capacity of each container when needed.
5. Describe precisely the experiment step by step and justify all your actions in the "add a note" section inside each action.

Make sure you work extremely precisely and record exactly what you are going to do. Sloppy work may result in the crime not being solved!

When you have finished, compare your experimental procedure with others. For this, use the search tool (on the bottom left corner of the screen).
