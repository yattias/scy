Concept Map for chemical analysis

Since you are a new chemical expert in the forensic SCY-Lab, your colleagues need to know if you understand your domain of expertise and the technique you are going to use: the thin layer chromatography (TLC).
Firts read the handbook section about chemistry. Then, make a concept map related to the domain and the techniques of chemical analysis.

There is a double goal in this activity:

  • Identify and organize the concepts related to the TLC
  • Connect the TLC to the other techniques that a chemical analyst might use.
Parts of the concept map are already given. Use given concepts and linking words to complete your concept map.
