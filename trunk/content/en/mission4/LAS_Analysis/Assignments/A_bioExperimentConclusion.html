Conclusion of the experiment

In order to ensure the reliability of results, experiments should always be performed more than once. Instead of that, compare your results with others. For this, use the search tool (on the bottom left corner of the screen) to retreive the "Results of the experiments" from your colleagues.

Based on your results and others, make your conclusion. Explain how and why you can make this conclusion.

When you have finished, ask your colleagues for feedback. To do that, open the "F" drawer.
