Now you have read the story about William and Kate and formulated an initial decision about the reasonability of growing genetically modified salmon in EU countries. Although, this problem seemed simple, there are actually several aspects, which need to be considered.

All students working with GMO module have been assigned to an expert role. There are four different roles: biologist, animal protector, economist and jurist. You are a jurist.

Your task is to think as a jurist and formulate the ELO Initial field-specific decision based on juridical arguments only. Please do not consider the arguments of other fields. Go on and explore the contents of the ELO drawers.

After you have formulated the ELO Initial field-specific decision, open the mission map and move next to the Revised field-specific space.

Learning goals:

After completion of this part of the mission:

• you can collect, organize and evaluate data on a definite subject;
• you can collect, organize and evaluate data on a definite subject;
• you can evaluate information about legal problems related to the application of gene technology;
• you take into account legislative aspects in decision making;
