Transgenic Animal Models May Increase Suffering for Research Animals

Experimenting may cause great suffering for research animals. One of the more common uses of bioengineering is for the purposeful creation of animals who are either diseased or afflicted with a predisposition to develop a fatal, genetic disease. Such research animals are purposefully created to suffer. The entire population of genetically engineered research animals exist in bodies whose inevitable fate is constant pain and unnaturally rapid deterioration. Animal rights organizations have always held that research animals are abused, badly mistreated, and without even the most basic of rights. And, only for the benefit of human welfare.
