What is the aim of legislation regulating GM food and feed?

In order to ensure that the development of modern biotechnology, more specifically of genetically modified organisms (GMOs), takes place in complete safety, the European Union has established a legal framework regulating genetically modified (GM) food and feed in the EU. This framework pursues the global objective of ensuring a high level of protection of human life and health and welfare, environment and consumer interests, whilst ensuring that the internal market works effectively.
