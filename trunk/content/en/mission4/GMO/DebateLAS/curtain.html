There are four different experts (biologists, economists, lawyers and ethics specialists) working on this GMO problem. In this space, you will work in a group – Expert council. Expert council consists of four members.

You have to open the ELO – Expert council’s decision and copy there your previously composed Revised field-specific decision.

Your task is to read all the decisions of different fields and hold an argued conversation about the pros and cons of the all fields’ experts’ arguments. Based on the conversation, you have to make changes and finalize the ELO.

After the formulation of your ELO Expert council’s decision, open the mission map and move next to the Final decision space.
