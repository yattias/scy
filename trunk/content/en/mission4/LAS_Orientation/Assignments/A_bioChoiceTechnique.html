Choice of techniques

Now that the goal of the analysis is set, you must select the forensic techniques that you will use to answer your research question.

  • Consult the handbook for a list of forensic specialities. Write down what your speciality is.
  • Read the table in the handbook that describes the various techniques that are related to your speciality.
  • Choose which sequence of techniques must be employed for your (forensic) research. For step 2 there are two possibilities (see the diagram on your right).
