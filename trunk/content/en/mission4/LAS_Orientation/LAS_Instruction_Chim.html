A number of ink samples are sent to your laboratory by the attorney general. These samples are all related to the same murder case (case number CY0347), and include one evidence sample and several reference samples.
The assignment of the attorney general is the following:

“We need to identify the author of an anonymous letter. This can be done by comparing inks. For this task, we provide you with the anonymous letter and several pens collected by the police.”

In this part of the mission you will justify the choice of the TLC technique to answer to this assignment.

Learning goals
After completion of this part of the mission,
  • you can explain how the technique of TLC works.
  • you can explain why TLC is a good technique to analyse inks.
