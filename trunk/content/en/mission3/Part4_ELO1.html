4.2 Energy fact sheet

Your body uses the energy in your food for different kinds of activities and processes. But the amount of energy you need depends on a number of factors. During this activity, you will collect information about these topics and put this in an ‘energy fact sheet’. The beginning of this fact sheet has been made for you [link to SCYLab tool with existing image, see Appendix A]. Follow the steps below to complete your fact sheet.

1. Watch the following video: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HLTzi6nGC2w.

2. Complete the upper part of the fact sheet. Drag the orange textboxes to the right places. Browse the internet (e.g. Wikipedia) for additional information when necessary.

3. Complete the middle part of the fact sheet. Drag the blue textboxes to the right places. For each category (‘movement of muscles’ and ‘other body processes’), think of one other example and write this down in one of the empty boxes. Browse the internet for additional information when necessary.

4. Complete the bottom part of the fact sheet. The amount of energy your body needs depends on a number of factors. For each factor (‘weight’, ‘age’ and ‘gender’), explain the relation with energy needs, just like has been done for the factor ‘activities’. Browse the internet for additional information when necessary.
