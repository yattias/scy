10: Calculate daily calorie intake

Now it is time to calculate your daily calorie intake. Use the food and exercise diary you created (in activity 04) and create a table like the example below (in SCY data processing tool):

  • Make an inventory of all food items you have consumed, and their amounts (for all three days combined).
  • Find the amount of calories per 100g for each item on the one of the websites listed below.
  • Use the data processing tool to calculate the amount of calories consumed (for each food item) and the total amount of calories.
  • Divide the resulting value by three in order to find the average daily amount of calories you consume.
  • Put this value in your health passport.
