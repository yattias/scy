Health problems: The goal of this mission is to create a healthy pizza. By now you must be familiar with the nutrient composition of a healthy diet. You have also learned that there should be a balance between the amount of calories in your diet and the amount of energy you spend. But what exactly are the consequences if your diet is not healthy? This part of the mission focuses on the most common diet-related health problems in our society.

Learning goals
In this part of the mission you will:

  • become aware of the importance of a balanced diet for their health.
  • become aware of the long-term consequences of unhealthy food.
