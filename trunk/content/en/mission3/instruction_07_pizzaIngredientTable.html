07: Pizza ingredient table

Please note that this activity is optional! Ask your teacher whether you have to do this assignment or not.

In the occasion of available time and appropriate equipment, you can collect the nutritional value information of the pizza ingredients and complete the table below on your own. You may visit your local supermarket/grocery store or carry out your search via the internet. While collecting the data, you can take paper notes or use available mobile data storing devices (e.g. cell-phones, palm-pilots, etc.). Since there are many ingredients on the table, it will be better to form groups and look up the information for some of the ingredients (e.g. 5-10 ingredients per group) or your teacher can provide a semi-complete table and you will have to complete the missing information. You will be able to use the complete table later on during the optimization strategy.
