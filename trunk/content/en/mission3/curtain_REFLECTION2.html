The optimization strategy: In this part of the mission you will create a second virtual pizza and discuss the steps you followed in doing so. Then you will be introduced to 'the optimization strategy' using a simple example. Finally you will have a chance to apply the optimization strategy to your healthy pizza.

Learning goals
In this part of the mission you will:

  • reflect on the methodology you followed while creating your healthy pizza.
  • learn about the optimization strategy.
  • implement the optimization strategy in order to create your final healthy pizza.
