Spending energy: In the previous part of the mission you have studied food pyramids. The food pyramid is designed to show the optimal balance between different categories of food products. But you probably remember the staircase at the left side of the new food pyramid: a reference to your exercising behaviour. It is not only the composition of your diet that matters, the quantity is also important. There has to be a balance between the amount of calories in your food, and the amount of energy you spend. And that depends on the activities you undertake throughout the day.

Learning goals
In this part of the mission you will:

  • become aware of the importance of regular exercise for their health.
  • learn how to explain how energy is measured.
  • learn to give examples of processes of the human body that require energy.
  • learn what factors the energy need of a person depend upon.
