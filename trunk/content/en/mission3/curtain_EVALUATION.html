Create and compare pizza's: Based on the optimization strategy, you have created your final healthy pizza. You will now compare all three pizzas you created throughout the mission. Finally, you will have the opportunity to compare all pizza's in the class and compete for the healthiest pizza among your peers.

Learning goals
In this part of the mission you will:

  • compare the pizza's you created throughout the mission and decide which one is the healthiest.
  • compare your final pizza with those of others.
  • learn how to write an advisory report for your school canteen.
