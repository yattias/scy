You have done research on the effects of energy drinks and answered the research question and hypothesis. Finally you will present your work in a report. It is very important that your report is complete and understandable. If it is not, other people, like the teacher or your classmates will not believe your results.

Learning goals
In this part of the mission you will learn:

  • how to describe your simulations
  • how to outline the main findings of your research
  • how to present your work
