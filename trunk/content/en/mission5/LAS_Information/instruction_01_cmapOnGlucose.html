01: Concept-map on glucose

The diagram in the window on the right is a concept-map. A concept-map shows how important concepts are related to one another. This concept-map is on glucose and the role of glucose in the human body. Your job is to finish this concept-map by filling in the empty boxes with the question marks. First, have a look at the resources (you can open the resource drawer with the tab indicated with an 'R'). Then fill in the empty boxes in the concept-map.
