Welcome to Run For Your Life!

During this mission you are going to study the effects of energy drinks. Energy drinks claim to make you more alert or give you a boost of energy to keep on going. The effects of these drinks are incredible if you have to believe the commercials (e.g. watch this video). But do energy drinks really give you more energy? In this mission you are going to act as a researcher and try to answer this question.

Also, you will explore the virtual environment - SCY-Lab - that is behind the mission. You will learn how to navigate through the various parts of the mission, and how to create and handle your own files and documents.

Have fun.
