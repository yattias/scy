02: Hypothesis

Consider the following four situations and predict what will happen with the blood glucose contentration (remove the words that do not apply):

  1. At time 0 you drink an energy drink containing glucose. After that you sit down for 60 minutes.
  2. At time 0 you drink an energy drink containing glucose. After 30 minutes you start running on a treadmill for 20 minutes.
  3. At time 0 you drink a glass of water. After 30 minutes you start running on a treadmill for 20 minutes.
  4. At time 0 you drink a glass of water. After that you sit down and don't move for 60 minutes.

Now read the following research question carefully:

Is it useful to drink an energy drink before you are going to exercise?

Perhaps you already have expectations about the answer to this research question. This expectation is your hypothesis. Formulate your own hypothesis by finishing the last sentence.
