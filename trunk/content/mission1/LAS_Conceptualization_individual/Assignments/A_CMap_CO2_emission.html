Concept map on CO2-levels

The goal of this mission is to design a CO2-friendly house. But why do we want our house to be CO2-friendly? The purpose of this assignment is to build a concept map that explains the importance of reducing global CO2-levels. Your concept map should also explain the most important processes of the carbon cycle.

A concept map is a graphical representation of important concepts (in boxes), and the relations between those concepts (represented by arrows or lines with a label). This may sound complicated, but if you take a look at the example on the following page, it will become clear what the main idea of a concept map is.

What do I have to do?

  1. A beginning of the concept-map is already given.
  2. You have to add 10 other concepts at least.
  3. Each concept has to be connected with at least one other concept with an arrow.
  4. Each connection (relation) has to be defined. Useful labels are for instance ‘is’, ‘causes’, ‘produces’ and ‘used for’.
