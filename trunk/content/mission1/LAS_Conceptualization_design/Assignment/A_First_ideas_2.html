What do I have to do?

1. To get you started, five items are already given. Begin by dragging these items to the appropriate place in the drawing, and connect them to the corresponding arrow.
2. Add as many new ideas as you want.
3. Make sure that each member of your group adds his/her ideas to the drawing.
4. Please remember that you cannot give wrong answers here. If you are not sure about whether your idea is really CO2-friendly, you simply add a question mark.
