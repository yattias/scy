Why reduce global CO2-levels?

The goal of this mission is to design a CO­2-friendly house. But why do we want our house to be CO2-friendly? The purpose of this assignment is to build a concept-map that explains the importance of reducing global CO2-levels. Your concept-map should also explain the role of the carbon cycle in regulating the climate on earth.

A concept-map is a graphical representation of important concepts (in boxes), and the relations between those concepts (represented by labelled arrows). This may sound complicated, but if you take a look at the example below, it will become clear what the main idea of a concept-map is.

The assignment:

  1. A beginning of the concept-map is already given.
  2. You have to add 10 other concepts at least.
  3. Each concept has to be connected with at least one other concept with an arrow.
  4. Each connection (relation) has to be defined. Useful labels are for instance ‘is’, ‘causes’, ‘produces’ and ‘used for’.
