Using the model
 

With this model, you can investigate your everyday electricity consumption.
Select consuming type: 'manually' – enables you to enter the consuming schedules for all devices manually; 'demo1' – the consuming schedules of all devices corresponds to the sustainable consuming; 'demo2' – the consuming schedules of all devices corresponds to the non-sustainable consuming.
Select 'Workday' or 'Weekend'.
When you have selected the consuming type 'manually', you have to enter the working schedules for all devices. When you have selected a consuming type 'demo1' or 'demo2', the schedules of all devices have been preselected but you can change them according to your needs.
In order to change a schedule of a device, click on the device's icon. You can choose up to three different consuming periods for one device. After that click on the 'Confirm'.
When a schedule has been set for a device, a red clock icon displays near to it .
You can change the power of the bulbs and the electric heaters/coolers as well (in watts).
Click 'Start' for starting the virtual electricity consumption for 24 h. Watch the model. When a device starts working, a green clock icon displays near to it . The power meter displays the electricity consumption separately in day and night time.
Use the notebook for taking notes.
Clicking on the 'Schedule' button displays the timetable of all devices. You can see the schedule of a device while moving the mouse over the clock icon near to this device.
To stop the model, click on the 'Stop'. To reset the clock and the power meter, click on the 'Back'.
To reset the model, click on the 'Restart'.
To save your results in the table for SCY-Lab, click on the 'Create ELO', enter your data, and click 'Save'.
